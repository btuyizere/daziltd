About DAZI Ltd

DAZI Ltd is a Rwandan agribusiness company specializing in the processing and trading of high-quality maize products, including kawunga flour and dry maize grains. With more than 12 years of experience, DAZI Ltd works directly with farmer cooperatives to improve post-harvest handling, storage, and value addition.

The company contributes to food security and farmer income growth across Western Rwanda and exports to neighboring countries like DR Congo.

Founded: 2011

RDB Registration Code (TIN): 102418962

Location: Rubengera Sector, Karongi District, Western Province, Rwanda
